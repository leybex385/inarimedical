About

Huang Shiyan is a Singaporean physician-scientist and healthcare executive with over 15 years of multinational experience spanning clinical medicine, biomedical research, and healthcare industry strategy.

Educated within Singapore’s leading medical institutions and the University of Oxford, her work integrates rigorous clinical training with advanced research in genomics and precision oncology.

Her professional focus is on translating clinical and scientific innovation into scalable, technology-enabled healthcare systems that improve cancer outcomes while reducing systemic inefficiencies.
